SALES & TRADING

Embark on a journey of financial success with our Sales & Trading services, where we seamlessly blend industry expertise, cutting-edge technology, and a client-centric approach to deliver unparalleled value.

Our products:

  • Oil products: Gardenium LTD is a wholesaler of oil products. With a strong presence in the energy sector, we meet diverse client needs across industries such as transportation, manufacturing, and power generation. As a trusted partner, we offer a comprehensive product portfolio including gasoline, diesel, fuel, liquefied petroleum gas and specialized petroleum derivatives. Our products are sourced from established refineries and manufacturers, adhering to industry regulations. Through strategic partnerships and a robust supply chain, we efficiently procure, store, and deliver oil products worldwide. The Company operates in strict compliance with international sanctions related to oil and oil products.
  • Agricultural products: Gardenium LTD specializes in the sale of high-quality agricultural products. We recognize the indispensable role that agro food plays in the international market. Our comprehensive product range includes grain wheat, corn, soy, barley, rapeseed and oilseeds, ensuring we can meet a wide range of requirements. With a strong foothold in the agricultural sector, we are a trusted supplier catering to the diverse requirements of farmers, agricultural businesses, and distributors.Being merchants of different grains and oilseeds Gardenium LTD connects producers and users of grains and oilseeds in different countries.
  • Fertilizers: Gardenium LTD, we specialize in trading top-notch fertilizers. Recognizing the pivotal role of fertilizers in maximizing yields, enhancing soil fertility, and boosting agricultural productivity, we offer an extensive product lineup tailored to meet the specific needs of crops. Our range includes nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium-based fertilizers, as well as organic and specialty blends. Quality takes precedence in our operations. By establishing strategic partnerships with reputable manufacturers and suppliers, we ensure the provision of fertilizers that adhere to rigorous quality and regulatory standards. This guarantees that our customers receive high-quality, efficient, and environmentally-friendly fertilizers. Our knowledgeable team possesses profound expertise in agriculture and stays abreast of the latest agronomic practices, plant nutrition technologies, and fertilizers.
