ASSETS MANAGEMENT

In our company, we believe that proper asset management is the key to success, resilience, and efficiency. We specialize in creating and implementing strategic approaches to optimize the value of assets and reduce risks for our clients.

Our asset management services include:

  • Investment opportunities: We constantly analyze superior risk-adjusted returns across a broad range of products that are suited to our investment style and risk profile. We are evaluating new investment ideas to best serve the needs and reach our investment goals and manage company's financial assets to maximize return on invested capital. Since 2016, Gardenium LTD has been investing in promising Ukrainian and European companies. Our portfolio includes companies in the field of microcredit, real estate, collection, processing and supply of scrap metal and other industries. Based on more than 10 years of experience in investments, we know how to select high-yield investments from a variety of market opportunities, help to establish internal business processes of portfolio companies, and know how to make a successful exit from an investment. We make investment decisions based on the specifics of the industry or a specific company.
  • Assessment and business due diligence: Calculation of the business value estimate and recommendations for its increase. Assessment of the company's financial condition, development of a financial business model and comprehensive preparation of the company in order to attract external financing.
  • Strategic consulting: We advise management, shareholders, investors and creditors on a wide range of issues related to strategic planning, business transformation, capitalization management of the company.
  • Financing: Whenever possible, we help our suppliers and customers manage their working capital through pre-export finance and trade credit. Alternatively, we strive to offer best terms of payment available in respective markets through bespoke financial arrangements.
  • Advisory services: Whenever required, we advise our customers on market trends, export-import regulations, tariffs and trade barriers, and other aspects of international trade to facilitate decision-making and nurture strategic relationships.

Our team is ready to be your reliable partner in implementing the best asset management practices. Reach out to us, and together we will make your assets more efficient, resilient, and prepared for future challenges.
